Process

Getting Started (Week 1)

Prepare the composting bed with cocopeat and organic waste. Introduce earthworms to their new environment and ensure regular watering to kickstart decomposition.

Busy Bees (Week 2)

Witness bustling microbial activity as earthworms actively consume and break down organic materials. Keep moisture levels in check.

Breaking It Down (Week 3)

Notice the composting bed volume shrinking, indicating advanced nutrient conversion. Regularly observe and adjust moisture levels.

Harvest Time (Week 4)

The compost turns rich and dark. Thriving earthworms contribute valuable microbial activity. Your matured organic fertilizer is ready for harvest!
